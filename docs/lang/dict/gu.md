Gu
---

The gu, as the main word in the category, has a symbol only consisting of the shape

### Igo

*Noun*

1. The speaker or speaking group.

   > gu igo guud \
   > *I am a good* **or** *We are goods*

2. One of the speakers (if `ipo` is attached).
